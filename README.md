# E-commerce Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This dashboard analyzes e-commerce sales performance across cities, product categories, and customer orders. It helps identify revenue trends, profitability drivers, cancellation patterns, and customer satisfaction factors to support business decision-making.

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses often face challenges such as:

* Identifying high-performing markets and product categories.
* Managing order cancellations.
* Improving customer satisfaction and ratings.
* Reducing operational inefficiencies caused by shipping delays.

Without proper analysis, these issues can impact revenue growth and customer retention.

## Project Objective

* Analyze sales and profitability performance across cities.
* Identify the most profitable product categories.
* Monitor order cancellations and customer ratings.
* Evaluate the impact of shipping delays on customer experience.

## Tools Used

* SQL + Google Sheets
* Data Visualization
* Pivot Analysis

## Key Findings

* Bangalore generates the highest revenue while maintaining strong profitability.

* Electronics is the most profitable category, contributing the largest share of total profit.

* Longer shipping delays are associated with lower customer ratings.

* Orders with shipping delays greater than 7 days experience significantly higher cancellation volumes.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory availability for highly profitable categories such as Electronics.

* Improve logistics performance to reduce shipping delays.

* Monitor delivery service levels to improve customer ratings and retention.

## Business Impact

* Provides visibility into revenue and profitability performance.

* Helps identify high-value product categories and markets.

* Supports management of cancellations and customer satisfaction.
